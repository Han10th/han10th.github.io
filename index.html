About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at City University of Hong Kong supervised by Raymond H. Chan and co-supervised by Xue-Cheng Tai, Jean-Michel Morel. I obtained my M.Phil. degree from Department of Mathematics at The Chinese University of Hong Kong supervised by Lok Ming Lui. I obtained my B.Sc degree from School of Mathematics at Sun Yat-Sen University.

Research Interests

  • Computational Fluid Mechanics: Fluid-Structure Interaction, Blood Flow Simulation
  • Computational Differential Geometry: Geometric Deep Learning
  • Scientific Machine Learning: PINN method, Neural Networks
  • Image Science: Image Segmentation, Interactive Segmentation
